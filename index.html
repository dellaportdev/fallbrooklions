About Us

Who We Are

We are a Lions Club in Fallbrook, CA, part of Lions International, a global service organization dedicated to making our communities better. Whether you’re a current member or new visitor, you’re welcome to learn more below.

A Lions Club is a volunteer organization that brings people from your local community together to plan and run hands-on projects — like collecting used eyeglasses, hosting food drives, or building community gardens.

Lions International

A Global Movement of Service

For over 100 years, Lions have served their local communities as a service organization. Local clubs are guided by eight global causes:

  • Childhood Cancer
  • Diabetes
  • Disaster Relief
  • Environment
  • Humanitarian
  • Hunger
  • Vision
  • Youth

To learn more, visit lionsclubs.org.
