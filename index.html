Donate Your Used Eyeglasses

Fallbrook Ranch Lions Club can take your used eyeglasses. One of the Lions Clubs' global causes is vision. Donated eyeglasses are first sorted and refurbished, then given free to patients that receive vision screenings around the county until a comprehensive eye exam and eye wear are obtained. We have 2 locations in town where glasses can be dropped off in our donation boxes:
Major Market - 845 S. Main Ave. Fallbrook
Fallbrook Community Center - 341 Heald Ln. Fallbrook

Lions International

For over 100 years, Lions have served their local communities as a service organization. Local clubs can find a variety of service projects that benefit their communities and also use the Lions' eight global causes to guide them:

  • Childhood cancer
  • Diabetes
  • Disaster relief
  • Environment
  • Humanitarian
  • Hunger
  • Vision
  • Youth

To learn more about Lions International, visit lionsclubs.org.
