Who we are

We are a new forming Lions Club in Fallbrook, CA. In order to be officially chartered, we need 20 members minimum. We are close to our goal and hope to be chartered by early 2025. Once that happens, we will discuss and find service projects that help the Fallbrook community.

Lions International

For over 100 years, Lions have served their local communities as a service organization. Local clubs can find a variety of service projects that benefit their communities and also use the Lions' eight global causes to guide them:

  • Childhood cancer
  • Diabetes
  • Disaster relief
  • Environment
  • Humanitarian
  • Hunger
  • Vision
  • Youth

To learn more about Lions International, visit lionsclubs.org.
